WHO WE
ARE

Vecron Exim was founded through collaboration between former trade industry executives, highly skilled financiers and successful Silicon Valley VCs. Having worked within and alongside these industries, we have accumulated deep understanding of the complications trade businesses and start-ups face when interacting with conventional banks. As an independent finance company with vast international trade and business experience, we are able to make prompt decisions that are aligned with our partners objectives, throughout the supply chain. Our diversified management integrates different industry experience and expertise, and provides a unique perspective from which we are able to customize an ideal solution.
